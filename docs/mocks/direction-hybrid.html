What was taken from where

From 2: the entire color world and mood — warm gunmetal surfaces, ember accent, scanline + ember top strip, glowing Orbitron nametags, beveled metal-gradient medal headers, tinted lanes, LED toggles, Rajdhani HUD type. The blunt cut corners are gone: every plate and panel now uses soft 8–10px radii (pills for toggles). From 1: the structure and density — compact one-row header (wordmark left, tabs inline, share+gear right), single toolbar card with labeled fields, 42px table rows, points bar (recolored ember→gold), modes as "first two + N". From 3: the podium strip under the header (2–1–3, champion elevated with a gold edge glow), reskinned in gunmetal and metal gradients. The page hero moment now comes from the podium, which is why the header could go compact. Mobile: podium leads (champion full-width, runners-up split), then direction 3's one-row-per-player list — rank, name, four metal dot-counts, points — no second line of game logos; games/modes detail lives on the player page. Filters stay a chip rail.

Implementation notes (MUI mapping)

  • Theme: palette primary = ember #e05a1f, warm dark surfaces; shape.borderRadius: 8–10 (no clip-paths anywhere — the cut corners are gone, so this is plain radius like direction 1); Rajdhani + Orbitron only.
  • Scanline + vignette + ember top strip = body-level globals in the slimmed App.css.
  • Medal headers keep full painted gradients in the existing gold-header… classes; lane tints + edges in the placement-cell classes. Zero emotion-hash selectors.
  • Points bar = ::after on the points cell, ember→gold gradient, width % of leader.
  • Flagged deviation (accepted): the podium strip is a small presentational block at the top of PlayerList rendering rows 1–3 it already computes (~40 lines JSX, no state/logic changes). It hides under Power-Ranking-irrelevant filters the same way rows do (it is just rows 1–3) and collapses to the mobile podium at phone widths.
  • LAN / POWER = pill toggles with LED dot (restyled Checkbox), selects/search/slider = MUI small-size with theme overrides.
